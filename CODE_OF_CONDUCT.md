# Code of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant. We are committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone involved in the Velo community.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- using welcoming and inclusive language,
- being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences,
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback,
- focusing on what is best for the community and the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory conduct,
- insulting or derogatory comments,
- trolling, baiting, or disruptive behavior,
- publishing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull requests that do not align with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

Please report unacceptable behavior by contacting the maintainers through the project’s private reporting channel described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to project spaces, public channels, issue trackers, pull requests, and other community interactions related to Velo.
