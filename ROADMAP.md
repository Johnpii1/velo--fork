# Roadmap

This roadmap captures the near-term priorities for Velo as an open-source project.

## Near Term

- harden the escrow and release flow,
- improve contract test coverage,
- connect the API layer to live contract interactions,
- expand mobile claim and QR experience,
- publish stronger operational and deployment guidance.

## Medium Term

- formalize the reputation and provider discovery model,
- integrate privacy-preserving identity and nullifier concepts,
- improve observability and deployment automation,
- strengthen security review and release processes.

## Long Term

- support broader payment and settlement integrations,
- expand cross-chain interoperability where appropriate,
- grow the contributor and maintainer community,
- establish a stable public release cadence.
